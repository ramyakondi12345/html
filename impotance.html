Impotance and History

The Constitution of India was adopted by the Constituent Assembly on 26th November, 1949, and came into effect on 26th January, 1950. The day of 26th
January was chosen as it was the anniversary of Purna Swaraj Day, which was celebrated on 26th January, 1930, as a day of independence from British colonial rule.
This important day marks the transition of India from a British colony to a republic. This day signifies unity and national pride.
